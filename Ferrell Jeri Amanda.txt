Miss Jeri Amanda Ferrell age 28 of Elkhart, Indiana formerly of Walker, KY departed this life on Monday August 22, 2011 in Indiana. She was born on March 31, 1983 in Williamson, West Virginia and was the daughter of Charlene (Messer) Ferrell and the late Jerry A. Ferrell. Amanda was a member of the Union Mission Baptist Church.

She is survived by her mother, Charlene Ferrell of Walker, KY, three daughters, Savannah Watts of Bell Co., Sydney Watts of Indiana and Kyhra Daniels of Barbourville, one son, Theron Wagers of Walker, one sister Lisa Clifton-Gregory and husband Jamie of Shepherdsville, KY , one brother Jeremy Ferrell of Walker and a nephew Jared Baker and wife Jennifer of Louisville, KY. Amanda also leaves behind a host of many other relatives and friends to mourn her passing.

Funeral services for Amanda Ferrell will be conducted in the Hampton Funeral Home Chapel on Friday, August 26, 2011 at 2:00 p.m. with Rev. Mickie Smith and Rev. David Mounce officiating. Pallbearers will be family & friends. Burial will be in the Messer Cemetery at Walker, KY. Hampton Funeral Home will be in charge of all arrangements.

Friends will be received at the Hampton Funeral Home on Friday from 11:00 a.m. until the hour of service at 2:00 p.m.
